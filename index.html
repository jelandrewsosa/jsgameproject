  • 1v1 Strategy turn based Card Game
  • 4 Types of cards(normal, magic, defense and magic spell cards)
    - Attack (uses normal attack card)
    - Magic attack (uses magic attack card)
    - Defense (variety of defensive card stance to self)
    - Magic Spell (variety of useful spells for player advantage and enemy player disadvantage)
  • 53 unique cards with corresponding tier list damage, defensive effects and magical spell effects
  • Attack and Magic Cards
    - tier1 (Green card does 2 damage)
    - tier2 (Yellow card does 4 damage)
    - tier3 (Blue card does 6 damage)
    - tier4 (Red card does 8 damage)
  • Defensive Cards (Defense Card only affects the next Enemy turn, Auto dispells self buff on your next turn)
    - Reflect Normal (Reflect Enemy Normal Attack)
    - Reflect Magic Damage (Reflect Enemy Magic Attack)
    - Dodge (Dodges Enemy Normal Attack)
    - Negate Magic Attack (Dodge Enemy Magic attack)
    - Shield (Half Enemy Normal attack)
    - Magic Shield (Half Enemy Magic attack)
  • Magic Spell Cards
    - Eliminate (Replace All Enemy Cards)
    - Manipulate (Replace own 4 Cards)
    - Heals 12 hp
    - Third Eye (Reveal 1 Random Enemy Card)
    - Draw (Draw 2 Cards, Note you must choose 1 card to discard on hand)
  • Each player will have 4 initial cards and 100hp
    - 4 max cards per player
    - a player must discard if card on hand will be more than 4
  • Player can only choose one command each turn
  • Player has only 30 seconds to perform a move or else that player auto skips the round
  • Auto removes card after use
  • Random first player turn(Not Implemented Yet)
  • Reset Deck if Deck count goes to 0
  • First player to lose ALL HP loses the game
